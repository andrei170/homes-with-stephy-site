02 — How it works

From "¿y ahora qué?" to keys in your hand.

Buying your first home can feel like a maze. Stephy turns it into four warm, simple steps - and walks every one of them beside you, in the language that feels like home.

Paso 01

Say hola

Nos conocemos

A relaxed first chat, on WhatsApp or a 15-minute call. No pressure, no jargon - just Stephy getting to know you, your family, and the home you are dreaming about.

Paso 02

Make a game plan

Tu plan, paso a paso

Together you map the budget, the lender, and the timeline - explained line by line so nothing is a surprise. You will always know exactly what comes next.

Paso 03

Tour real homes

Vemos casas juntas

Stephy lines up homes that actually fit you and walks each one at your pace. Honest read on every property - the good, the "let's keep looking," all of it.

Paso 04

Celebrate the keys

¡Bienvenida a casa!

Offer, closing table, and the moment those keys land in your hand. Stephy is there for the paperwork - and there for the photo when it is finally yours.
