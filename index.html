03 — Meet Stephy

A compassionate agent, tailoring home experiences.

Stephy Salazar is an Austin real estate agent with eXp Realty who does this work for one reason: she loves watching a family walk into a home that is finally theirs. Her whole approach is built around care - listening first, moving at your pace, and treating your home search like it is her own.

She is bilingual in English and Spanish, and she is building something rare in this business: a real community of women learning about home ownership together. When you work with Stephy, you do not just get an agent. You get someone in your corner, and a whole group at your back.
